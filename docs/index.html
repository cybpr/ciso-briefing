The Axios maintainer confirmed the compromise was the result of a highly targeted social engineering campaign by North Korean threat actors (UNC1069), who tailored their efforts specifically to him by cloning the founders' likeness of a legitimate, well-known company. All organizations must immediately audit for compromised Axios versions, rotate all credentials from potentially affected systems, and treat any installation of these versions as a full system compromise.
